## Overview
Bookiji ("we", "us", or "our") operates a universal booking platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email, name, phone number
- **Profile Data**: Professional information, service preferences
- **Location Data**: Geographic location (with explicit consent)
- **Booking Information**: Appointment details, service preferences
- **Payment Information**: Transaction records (not actual card data)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Usage Data**: IP address, access times, pages viewed
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies**: Web beacons, pixels
- **Location Information**: GPS, WiFi access points, cell towers (with consent)

## How We Use Your Information
- Process and manage bookings
- Provide customer support
- Send service notifications
- Improve our platform
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

To learn more about how Google uses data, visit: [How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/)
