# Bookiji Pilot User Guide

## Welcome to Bookiji Beta!

Thank you for being an early pilot user. Your feedback is invaluable in shaping Bookiji into the best booking platform possible.

## What is Bookiji?

Bookiji is a **universal booking platform** that connects customers with service providers in real-time. Whether you need a plumber, a tutor, or a massage therapist, Bookiji helps you find and book services instantly.

## Key Features

### For Customers

1. **AI-Powered Search**
   - Describe what you need in natural language
   - Get instant matches with available providers
   - See real-time availability

2. **$1 Commitment Fee**
   - Low barrier to entry
   - Automated refunds if service isn't completed
   - No hidden fees

3. **Real-Time Booking**
   - See available slots immediately
   - Book and confirm in seconds
   - Get instant confirmation

4. **Provider Privacy**
   - Providers' exact locations are protected
   - You see service area, not exact address
   - Privacy-first design

### For Service Providers

1. **Easy Onboarding**
   - Set up your profile in minutes
   - Add services and availability
   - Start accepting bookings

2. **Privacy Protection**
   - Your exact location is never shared
   - Service area abstraction
   - Control your visibility

3. **Automated Payments**
   - Stripe integration
   - Automatic payouts
   - Transparent fees

4. **Booking Management**
   - Calendar integration
   - Real-time notifications
   - Customer communication

## Getting Started

### As a Customer

1. **Sign Up**
   - Create your account
   - Choose your role (Customer)
   - Complete your profile

2. **Search for Services**
   - Use the AI chat interface
   - Describe what you need
   - Browse available providers

3. **Book a Service**
   - Select a provider
   - Choose a time slot
   - Confirm your booking
   - Pay the $1 commitment fee

4. **Complete Your Booking**
   - Show up at the scheduled time
   - Receive your service
   - Leave a review

### As a Service Provider

1. **Sign Up**
   - Create your account
   - Choose your role (Provider)
   - Complete your profile

2. **Set Up Your Services**
   - Add service types
   - Set pricing
   - Define service areas

3. **Set Your Availability**
   - Add available time slots
   - Sync with your calendar
   - Set recurring availability

4. **Go Live**
   - Make your profile active
   - Start receiving bookings
   - Manage your schedule
