# Post-Incident Review (Blameless)

This document exists to improve the system, not to assign blame.

Every SEV-1 or higher incident must have a postmortem.

---

## Incident Summary
- Incident ID:
- Date / Time:
- Severity:
- Incident Commander:

## What Happened (Timeline)
Describe the sequence of events factually.
Use Jarvis timelines when available.

## Impact
- Who was affected?
- What could not be done?
- Duration of impact

## Root Cause
What *system condition* allowed this to happen?
(Not "who made a mistake".)

## Detection
- How was the incident detected?
- Could it have been detected earlier?

## Response
- What went well?
- What slowed us down?
- What was confusing?

## Missing Guardrails
What protection could have prevented or limited this incident?

## Action Items
Each item must have:
- Description
- Owner
- Priority
- Target date

## Follow-Up
- Was the action completed?
- Did it reduce risk?

---

## Blameless Principle
Human error is treated as a signal that the system needs improvement.
