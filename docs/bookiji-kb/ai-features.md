# AI Features and Capabilities

Bookiji's AI system provides intelligent assistance throughout your booking experience.

## AI-Powered Service Matching

### Smart Provider Recommendations
Our AI analyzes your specific needs and matches you with the best available providers based on:
- **Service requirements**: Exact service type and specifications
- **Location preferences**: Proximity and service area coverage
- **Availability**: Real-time calendar integration
- **Provider ratings**: Customer feedback and success metrics
- **Specialization**: Provider expertise and certifications

### Natural Language Understanding
- **Conversational interface**: Describe what you need in plain English
- **Context awareness**: AI remembers your preferences and history
- **Multi-language support**: Available in English and French
- **Intent recognition**: Understands complex service requests

## AI Chat Assistant

### 24/7 Support
- **Instant responses**: Get answers to questions immediately
- **Knowledge base access**: Pulls from our comprehensive help articles
- **Booking guidance**: Step-by-step assistance through the process
- **Problem resolution**: Troubleshoot issues with AI guidance

### Personalized Experience
- **User history**: Remembers your previous interactions
- **Preference learning**: Adapts to your communication style
- **Proactive suggestions**: Offers relevant options before you ask
