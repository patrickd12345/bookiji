# Getting Started with Bookiji

Welcome to Bookiji! This guide walks you through the core concepts and helps you get up and running quickly.

## What is Bookiji?

Bookiji is a universal booking platform that connects customers with service providers in real-time. We use AI to match the right provider with your needs and guarantee your bookings.

## Key Features

- **$1 Commitment Fee**: We only hold $1 to verify your payment method - no hidden charges
- **AI-Powered Matching**: Our AI finds the best providers for your specific needs
- **Instant Booking**: Book appointments in seconds with real-time availability
- **Guaranteed Service**: We stand behind every booking with our satisfaction guarantee

## Getting Started as a Customer

### 1. Create Your Account
- Sign up with your email or social media account
- Complete your profile with preferences and contact information
- Add a payment method (we'll verify with a $1 hold)

### 2. Find and Book Services
- Search for services in your area
- Use our AI chat to describe what you need
- Browse provider profiles and reviews
- Book instantly with real-time availability

### 3. Manage Your Bookings
- View upcoming appointments in your dashboard
- Receive reminders and updates
- Reschedule or cancel if needed (24-hour notice for full refunds)

## Getting Started as a Provider

### 1. Provider Onboarding
- Complete your business profile
- Connect your calendar (Google, Apple, Outlook)
- Set your services and pricing
- Upload business documents for verification

### 2. Go Live
- Set your availability and working hours
- Enable instant booking for customers
- Start receiving booking requests

### 3. Manage Your Business
- View and confirm incoming bookings
- Manage your calendar and availability
- Track earnings and performance
- Receive weekly payouts
