# Bookiji Beta User Feedback Survey

---

## Thank you for helping us build the future of booking!

Your feedback will directly shape Bookiji's features and experience. Please take a few minutes to share your thoughts.

---

### 1. Onboarding & First Impressions
- How easy was it to sign up and get started? (1-5)
- What, if anything, was confusing or missing?

### 2. Booking Experience
- How easy was it to find and book a service? (1-5)
- Did you use chat, voice, or search? Which did you prefer?
- Was the $1 commitment fee clear and reasonable?
- Did you feel confident your booking was guaranteed?

### 3. AI Conversational Interface
- Did you try the AI chat or voice assistant?
- How natural and helpful was the AI? (1-5)
- What could make the AI experience better?

### 4. Loyalty & Rewards
- Did you notice Bookiji Points, badges, or rewards?
- Would loyalty/rewards make you more likely to use Bookiji again?

### 5. General Feedback & Suggestions
- What did you like most about Bookiji?
- What would you change or improve?
- Any bugs, issues, or ideas for new features?
